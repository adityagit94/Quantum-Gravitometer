# Atom-Interferometry Reviewer Contacts for qgrav (Quantum-Gravitometer) - JOSS Physics Review

**Purpose:** Independent physics review of the open-source atom-interferometric gravimetry simulation package `qgrav` (https://github.com/adityagit94/Quantum-Gravitometer), which patches `bleykauf/aisim`. Regression benchmarks: Freier 2016, Hu 2013, Ménoret 2018.

**Compiled:** 28 May 2026. All emails listed below are verbatim from official institutional/group webpages or from corresponding-author footnotes in peer-reviewed papers, unless explicitly flagged as "inferred from institutional pattern, not directly verified."
